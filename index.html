Our Services

Thermal Insulation Services

Sharad Insulations offers thermal insulation solutions designed to improve energy efficiency in buildings. These services are tailored to meet client-specific needs and ensure optimal indoor climate control.

VRF Systems Installation

As a Toshiba channel partner, they install VRF systems that allow precise temperature control in different areas. These systems are ideal for large commercial buildings, enhancing comfort and energy efficiency.

Interior Design & Furniture Solutions

Their interior services include custom furniture and aesthetic design for both commercial and residential spaces, ensuring a balance of functionality and style in all projects.

HVAC After-Sales Services

Sharad Insulations ensures ongoing support for all HVAC installations, providing maintenance and troubleshooting to keep systems running efficiently, with a dedicated team of service professionals.

Electrical Wiring & Electrification

They handle internal and external electrical wiring for buildings, ensuring safety and compliance with regulations while providing reliable power distribution.

LT Switchgear and Cabling Solutions

SAP Electro Contracts specializes in installing low-tension (LT) switchgear and cabling systems, critical for power distribution in industrial and commercial projects.

Fire Fighting Systems Installation

SSA Projects specializes in installing fire fighting systems, including hydrants, sprinklers, and alarms, ensuring safety and compliance with regulatory standards for all types of buildings.

Pump and Water Treatment Systems

The company installs pumps and water treatment systems, critical for maintaining water quality and efficient distribution in residential and commercial complexes.
